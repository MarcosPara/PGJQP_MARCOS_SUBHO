Metaphor

A metaphor is a mapping from one domain to another. For example, moo is a cow as beep is to car. These mapping get so buried in our language that we don't even notice their effects.

For examples, take the language used to described a debate:

  "He shot down her ideas."
  "She defended her position."
  "She overwhelmed his arguements"
  "He had to retreat from that position."

These are all debate-as-war metaphors. It's a zero sum game. Some wins and someone loses. The idea that both people could learn from each other just doesn't come up.
